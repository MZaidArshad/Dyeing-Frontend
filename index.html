About US

Who We Are?

Experts Dyeing & Bleaching equipped with sheer drive for achieving perfection in every sphere, has been moving from strength to strength, since its inception in 1983 by its Founder Proprietor Mr. Peer Muhammad, who is now being supported by his Sons along with a core team of knowledge enriched professionals. Experts Dyeing & Bleaching is fully integrated Terry Towelling Unit with capacity of 1,140,000 Kgs Terry Towels per anum.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce value added products of utmost standard for quality conscious global markets by utilizing the best available knowledge and technology. In our pursuit for global excellence, we have an in-house Dyeing and Bleaching facilities to achieve a consistent and competitive quality product.
